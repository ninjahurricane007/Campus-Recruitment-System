Features

Features

Discover the power of streamlined hiring with our campus recruitment system

Job Postings

Employers can create and post job openings, including job descriptions and requirements, to attract top talent.

Applicant Tracking

Employers can manage job applications, review resumes, and communicate with candidates throughout the recruitment process.

Resume Database

The system includes a database of student resumes, enabling employers to easily search and find qualified candidates.

Analytics

The system provides employers with data and insights on recruitment performance, such as application rates, candidate demographics, and more.

Student Resources

The system may offer resources such as career advice, interview tips, and resume building tools to help students enhance their job search.

Automated Communications

The system can send automated email or text message reminders, updates, and notifications to both employers and candidates.
